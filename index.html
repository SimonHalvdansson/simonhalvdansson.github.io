Simon Halvdansson

I am a PhD candidate at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) in Trondheim, studying under the supervision of Professor Franz Luef. My research is in harmonic analysis, functional analysis and operator theory - more specifically on time-frequency analysis using the framework of quantum harmonic analysis.
