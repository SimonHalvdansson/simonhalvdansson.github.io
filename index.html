Simon Halvdansson

I am a PhD candidate at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) in Trondheim, studying under the supervision of Professor Franz Luef. My research is in time frequency analysis using tools from harmonic analysis, functional analysis and operator theory. More specifically I have an interest in the framework of quantum harmonic analysis.
