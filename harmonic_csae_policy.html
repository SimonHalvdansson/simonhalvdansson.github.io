Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

Harmonic for Hacker News maintains a zero-tolerance stance against any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any creation, distribution, or promotion of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) is strictly prohibited.

Our app solely interfaces with the Hacker News API to read and post comments and links; it does not host or store user-generated CSAE content. If you encounter any content that appears to involve CSAE, please report it immediately to Hacker News via the in-app reporting feature.
